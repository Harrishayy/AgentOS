## Integration notes (from Harrish's doc)

- The Go side reads `bpf/common.h` byte-identically; if it drifts on
  P1's side we re-vendor `daemon/bpf/common.h.reference` from `bpf/common.h`.
- The daemon expects four `.bpf.o` objects at `--bpf-dir`. Defaults to
  `/usr/lib/agent-sandbox/bpf/`. Mehul's Makefile installs to
  `/usr/lib/agentsandbox/bpf/` (no dash). Reconcile by editing Mehul's
  Makefile to emit the dashed path (so they match Harrish's default).
- Eight programs by name (`asb_socket_connect`, `asb_sendto`, ...) — these
  must be present in Mehul's compiled objects.
- `bump MAX_POLICIES from 32 → 64` (Harrish ask). Apply during P2 merge by
  editing `bpf/common.h`.
